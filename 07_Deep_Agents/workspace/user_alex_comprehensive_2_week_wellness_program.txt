## Emergency Protocols

### High Stress Day:
- Use 5-4-3-2-1 grounding technique
- Quick 2-minute triangle breathing
- Simplify meals: Use pre-prepped options
- Gentle movement instead of full workout if needed
- Extended evening wind-down routine

### Poor Sleep Night:
- Don't abandon routine - consistency is key
- Light stretching instead of full workout next day
- Focus on hydration and protein at meals
- Extra mindfulness practice during day
- Earlier bedtime following evening

### Meal Prep Behind Schedule:
- Use simple options: Greek yogurt with nuts, avocado toast
- Frozen vegetables for quick stir-fries
- Keep emergency snacks handy
- Focus on protein inclusion at each meal
- Don't let one imperfect day derail the week

## Beyond Week 2: Sustainability Plan

### Habits to Maintain:
1. **Exercise:** Continue 3x/week schedule, gradually increase intensity
2. **Nutrition:** Weekly meal prep, focus on variety and protein
3. **Mindfulness:** Daily practice, even if just 5 minutes
4. **Sleep:** Non-negotiable consistent bedtime/wake time

### Monthly Reviews:
- Assess which strategies work best for your lifestyle
- Adjust meal variety based on preferences
- Progress exercise difficulty gradually
- Consider adding new stress management techniques

### Professional Support:
- Consider a registered dietitian for advanced nutrition planning
- Personal trainer for exercise progression after mastering basics
- Therapist or counselor for ongoing stress management
- Sleep specialist if sleep issues persist
