### 2-Week Vegetarian Nutrition Plan for User Alex

#### Overview:
This comprehensive meal plan is designed for a busy lifestyle, focusing on vegetarian meals that improve overall diet quality, include sufficient protein sources, and are easy to prepare. Each week includes breakfast, lunch, dinner, and healthy snacks with hydration and supplement recommendations at the end.

### Week 1:

#### Day 1:
- **Breakfast:** Overnight oats with almond milk, chia seeds, and berries.
- **Lunch:** Chickpea salad with cucumber, tomatoes, and lemon-tahini dressing.
- **Dinner:** Quinoa stir-fry with mixed vegetables and tofu.
- **Snacks:** Hummus with celery sticks.

#### Day 2:
- **Breakfast:** Smoothie with spinach, banana, and peanut butter.
- **Lunch:** Lentil soup with whole-grain bread.
- **Dinner:** Vegetable curry with brown rice.
- **Snacks:** Greek yogurt with honey and walnuts.

#### Day 3:
- **Breakfast:** Whole grain toast with avocado and poached egg.
- **Lunch:** Spinach and feta stuffed pita with side salad.
- **Dinner:** Black bean tacos with avocado and salsa.
- **Snacks:** Mixed nuts.

#### Day 4:
- **Breakfast:** Chia pudding with coconut milk and sliced mango.
- **Lunch:** Quinoa salad with roasted vegetables.
- **Dinner:** Stuffed bell peppers with rice and beans.
- **Snacks:** Carrot sticks with almond butter.

#### Day 5:
- **Breakfast:** Smoothie bowl with granola, banana, and berries.
- **Lunch:** Caprese salad (tomato, mozzarella, basil) with balsamic glaze.
- **Dinner:** Spinach and ricotta ravioli with marinara sauce.
- **Snacks:** Air-popped popcorn.

#### Day 6:
- **Breakfast:** Overnight oats with almond milk, cinnamon, and sliced apple.
- **Lunch:** Falafel wrap with tahini sauce and mixed greens.
- **Dinner:** Zucchini noodles with pesto and cherry tomatoes.

#### Day 7:

- **Snacks:** Edamame.

### Nutritional Guidance:
- **Protein Sources:** Incorporate beans, lentils, tofu, tempeh, nuts, seeds, Greek yogurt, and whole grains in meals to ensure adequate protein intake.
- **Hydration:** Aim for at least 8-10 cups of water daily. Herbal teas and infused water can also be included.
- **Supplements:** Consider a B12 supplement and omega-3 (from algae oil) to complement a vegetarian diet.

### Meal Prep Tips:
- Prepare grains (quinoa, rice) and legumes (beans, lentils) in bulk at the start of the week.
- Pre-chop vegetables and portion snacks for easy access during the week.
