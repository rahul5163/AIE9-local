# 2-Week Beginner-Friendly Exercise Program for User Alex

## Overview
- **Duration:** 2 Weeks
- **Frequency:** 3 Workouts per Week
- **Workout Time:** 30 Minutes Each
- **Goals:** Build consistency in exercising, enhance strength, cardio fitness, and flexibility.

## Equipment Recommendations
- Yoga mat (optional for comfort)
- Resistance bands (optional, for variety)
- Water bottle

## Tips for Maintaining Consistency and Motivation
1. **Set Reminders:** Schedule your workouts in advance and set reminders.
2. **Track Progress:** Keep a journal of your workouts to track improvement.
3. **Stay Positive:** Focus on the benefits of exercise for your health and well-being.
4. **Workout Buddy:** Consider finding a workout buddy for support and motivation.

## Week 1
### Day 1: Strength & Flexibility
- **Warm-up (5 min):**
  - Arm circles: 1 min
  - Hip circles: 1 min
  - March in place: 3 min

- **Workout (20 min overall):**
  1. Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 10 reps (1 min rest between sets)
  2. Push-ups (knee or standard): 2 sets of 8-10 reps (1 min rest)
  3. Plank: 2 sets of 20 seconds hold (30 seconds rest)
  4. Seated Forward Bend: Hold for 30 seconds

- **Cool Down (5 min):**
  - Child's Pose: 1 min
  - Standing Quad Stretch: 30 seconds each leg

### Day 2: Cardio & Core
- **Warm-up (5 min):**
  - High knees: 1 min
  - Arm swings: 1 min
  - Side lunges: 3 min

- **Workout (20 min overall):**
  1. Step-ups (on a sturdy box or stairs): 2 sets of 10 reps each leg (1 min rest)
  2. Mountain Climbers: 2 sets of 30 seconds (30 seconds rest)
  3. Bird-Dog: 2 sets of 8 reps per side (1 min rest)
  4. Side Plank: 15 seconds on each side (2 sets)

- **Cool Down (5 min):**
  - Cat-Cow Stretch: 1 min
  - Hamstring Stretch: 30 seconds each leg

### Day 3: Rest & Recovery

### Day 4: Strength & Endurance
- **Warm-up (5 min):**
  - Jumping jacks: 1 min
  - Dynamic chest opener: 1 min
  - Lateral leg swings: 3 min

- **Workout (20 min overall):**
  1. Lunges (forward or reverse): 2 sets of 8-10 reps each leg (1 min rest)
  2. Tricep Dips (on a chair/bench): 2 sets of 8-10 reps (1 min rest)
  3. Glute Bridges: 2 sets of 10 reps (1 min rest)
  4. Cobra Stretch: Hold for 30 seconds

- **Cool Down (5 min):**
  - Seated Twist: 30 seconds each side
  - Shoulder Stretch: 30 seconds each arm

### Day 5: Cardio & Flexibility
- **Warm-up (5 min):**
  - Skaters: 1 min
  - Arm circles: 1 min
  - Jogging in place: 3 min

- **Workout (20 min overall):**
  1. High Knees: 2 sets of 30 seconds (30 seconds rest)
  2. Burpees (modified if necessary): 2 sets of 5-8 reps (1 min rest)
  3. Finger to Toe Touch: Hold for 30 seconds
  4. Lying Oblique Twist: 30 seconds each side

- **Cool Down (5 min):**
  - Standing Forward Fold: Hold for 1 min

### Day 6: Rest & Recovery

### Day 7: Light Activity (optional)
- Suggestions: Gentle yoga, walking for 20-30 minutes, or light stretching.

## Week 2
### Day 8: Strength & Flexibility (Progression)
- **Warm-up (5 min):**
  - Dynamic stretches (light jogging, arm swings)

- **Workout (20 min overall):**
  1. Elevated Heel Squats (change elevation): 3 sets of 12 reps (1 min rest)
  2. Push-ups (standard or knee with higher reps): 3 sets of 10-12 reps (1 min rest)
  3. Plank: 3 sets of 25 seconds hold (30 seconds rest)
  4. Standing Forward Bend Stretch: Hold for 30 seconds

- **Cool Down (5 min):**
  - Downward Dog: Hold for 1 min

### Day 9: Cardio & Core (Progression)
- **Warm-up (5 min):**
  - Jumping jacks: 1-2 min

- **Workout (20 min overall):**
  1. Step-ups: 3 sets of 12 reps each leg (1 min rest)
  2. Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 40 seconds (30 seconds rest)
  3. Bird-Dog: 3 sets of 10 reps per side (1 min rest)
  4. Side Plank: 20 seconds on each side (2 sets)

- **Cool Down (5 min):**
  - Cat-Cow Stretch: 1 min

### Day 10: Rest & Recovery

### Day 11: Strength & Endurance (Progression)
- **Warm-up (5 min):**
  - Arm circles & leg swings
  
- **Workout (20 min overall):**
  1. Lunges: 3 sets of 10-12 reps each leg (1 min rest)
  2. Tricep Dips: 3 sets of 10-12 reps (1 min rest)
  3. Glute Bridges with one leg raised (alternate): 3 sets of 8 reps (1 min rest)
  4. Cobra Stretch: Hold for 30 seconds

- **Cool Down (5 min):**
  - Seated Twist: 30 seconds each side

### Day 12: Cardio & Flexibility (Progression)
- **Warm-up (5 min):**
  - Skaters & high knees

- **Workout (20 min overall):**
  1. High Knees: 3 sets of 40 seconds (30 seconds rest)
  2. Burpees: 3 sets of 6-8 reps (1 min rest)
  3. Finger to Toe Touch: Hold for 30-45 seconds
  4. Lying Oblique Twist: 30-45 seconds each side

- **Cool Down (5 min):**
  - Standing Forward Fold: Hold for 1 min

### Day 13: Rest & Recovery

### Day 14: Reflect & Plan Ahead
- **Activity:** Reflect on the past two weeks, note progress, and adjust goals for future exercises.
