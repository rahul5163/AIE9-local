# 30-Day Stress Management & Sleep Improvement Plan

## Week 1: Foundation & Sleep Hygiene

### Daily Morning Routine (7:00-8:00 AM)
- **7:00 AM**: Wake up, drink a glass of water
- **7:15 AM**: Gentle stretching (5-10 minutes)
- **7:25 AM**: Energizing breathing (4-7-8 method, 5 minutes)
- **7:30 AM**: Mindfulness practice (5 minutes)
- **7:45 AM**: Healthy breakfast

### Daytime Stress Management (9:00 AM-5:00 PM)
- Mindful breaks every hour (5 minutes)
- Afternoon gratitude journaling (5 minutes)

### Evening Wind-Down (8:00-10:00 PM)
- **8:00 PM**: Begin wind-down (no screens, ambient lighting)
- **9:30 PM**: Prepare for bed
- **10:00 PM**: Consistent bedtime

## Week 2: Building Consistency

### Morning Routine Enhancement
- Increase mindfulness to 10 minutes
- Add dynamic stretching
- Continue energizing breathing

### Daytime Practices
- Longer mindfulness sessions (10-15 minutes)
- Progressive muscle relaxation between tasks

### Evening Routine
- Continue wind-down activities
- Add guided meditation (10 minutes)

## Week 3: Intermediate Techniques

### Morning Routine (15-20 minutes total)
- Include visualizations during mindfulness
- Extend breathing exercises
- Morning affirmations

### Daytime Practices
- Progressive muscle relaxation
- Stress-reduction techniques
- Mindful transitions between activities

### Evening Routine
- Extended wind-down with journaling (10 minutes)
- Bedtime meditation
- Sleep environment optimization

## Week 4: Advanced Integration

### Morning Routine (20 minutes)
- Comprehensive visualization practice
- Advanced breathing techniques
- Intention setting for the day

### Daytime Practices
- 15-20 minute mindfulness sessions
- Weekly yoga or tai chi
- Stress response awareness training

### Evening Routine
- Complete relaxation ritual
- Gratitude and wellness review
- Advanced meditation techniques
