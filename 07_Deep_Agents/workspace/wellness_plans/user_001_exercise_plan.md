### **Weeks 1-2: Foundation Building**

**Daily Morning Energizing Routines:**
- **Day 1-7:** 
  - 5-minute warm-up (gentle stretching and marching in place)
  - 10-minute cardio burst (choose from: jumping jacks, high knees, or jogging in place)
  - 10-minute strength and flexibility combo:
    - 10 bodyweight squats (modify by holding onto a wall)
    - 10 push-ups (knee push-ups if needed)
    - 10 seated forward bends (for flexibility)
    - 1-minute standing or seated deep breathing with arm raises

**Weekly Overview:**
- **Monday:** (Strength Focus)
  - Morning: Above routine
  - Evening: Gentle yoga (stretching major muscle groups)
  
- **Wednesday:** (Cardio Focus)
  - Morning: Above routine
  - Evening: 15-minute brisk walk or low-impact cycling
  
- **Friday:** (Flexibility Focus)
  - Morning: Above routine
  - Evening: Stretching routine (focus on hamstrings, quads, shoulders)

- **Saturday:** (Hybrid)
  - Morning: 15 minutes of dance or your favorite fun activity
  - Evening: Light stretching and meditation

**Energy Improvement Tips:**
- Hydrate as soon as waking up
- Consider light snacks (like fruits) after workout

### **Weeks 3-4: Progressive Challenges**

**Daily Morning Energizing Routines:**
- **Day 15-30:** 
  - 5-minute dynamic warm-up (arm circles, leg swings)
  - 10-minute higher intensity cardio (burpees, mountain climbers)
  - 10-minute strength exercises: 
    - 12 lunges (alternating legs with or without weights)
    - 10 tricep dips (use a chair)
    - 10 glute bridges
    - 30-second side planks (modified if needed)

**Weekly Overview:**
- **Monday:** 
  - Morning: Above routine
  - Evening: Full body stretch (yoga or Pilates)

- **Wednesday:** 
  - Morning: Above routine
  - Evening: 20-minutes of interval training (1 min high intensity/2 min low intensity)

- **Friday:**
  - Morning: Above routine
  - Evening: 15-minute walk followed by foam rolling

- **Saturday:**
  - Morning: 15-minute HIIT workout (mix short bursts of cardio with strength)
  - Evening: Reflective stretching and breathing

**Energy Improvement Tips:**
- Maintain consistent sleep schedule (7-8 hours)
- Limit electronic distractions an hour before bed
- Ingest complex carbs (like oatmeal or whole grains) as pre-workout fuel

---

### **Evening Recovery Exercises:**
- **Gentle stretches** before bed focusing on major muscle groups (10-15 minutes)
- **Breathing exercises:** 5 minutes of deep and calming breaths to reduce stress
- **Foam rolling** for muscle relaxation

### **Tips for Exercise Timing:**
- Aim for morning workouts to kickstart energy levels for the day
- Evening types should focus more on relaxation rather than intensity to prepare the body for sleep
- Avoid heavy meals at least 1-2 hours before exercising, especially at night
