# 30-Day Exercise Plan to Boost Morning Energy Levels

## Overview
- **Frequency**: 3-4 sessions per week
- **Duration**: Each session is â‰¤30 minutes
- **Goals**: Improve energy levels, mix of cardio, strength, flexibility

## Weekly Schedule
### Week 1
- **Monday** (Cardio & Flexibility)
  - 10 min brisk walk or jog
  - 10 min stretching (focus on hamstrings, quadriceps, shoulders)
  - 5 min deep breathing exercises

- **Wednesday** (Strength & Energy Boost)
  - 5 min warm-up (marching in place)
  - 15 min bodyweight strength exercises: squats, push-ups, lunges (2 sets of 8-12 reps)
  - 5 min balance exercises (single-leg stands)

- **Friday** (Active Recovery)
  - 30 min yoga or gentle stretching

### Week 2
- **Monday** (Increase Cardio & Flexibility)
  - 12 min brisk walk or jog
  - 12 min stretching (include hip flexors)
  - 6 min deep breathing exercises

- **Wednesday** (Increase Strength Intensity)
  - 5 min warm-up
  - 18 min bodyweight exercises: squats, push-ups, lunges (3 sets of 8-12 reps)
  - 7 min core exercises (planks, bird dogs)

- **Friday** (Rest Day Activity)
  - Light activity: 20-30 min walk, leisurely bike ride

### Week 3
- **Monday** (Cardio Focus)
  - 15 min interval walking (1 min fast, 2 min slow)
  - 10 min dynamic stretching
  - 5 min mindfulness breathing

- **Wednesday** (Strength Focus)
  - 5 min warm-up
  - 20 min circuit: squats, push-ups, lunges, planks (3 sets, max reps)
  - 5 min balance & mobility (leg swings, arm circles)

- **Friday** (Active Recovery)
  - 30 min restorative yoga or Pilates

### Week 4
- **Monday** (Endurance Cardio & Flexibility)
  - 20 min brisk walk or jog
  - 5 min full-body stretch
  - 5 min deep breathing and mindfulness

- **Wednesday** (Strength Endurance)
  - 5 min warm-up
  - 25 min strength circuit: increase reps and weights (if applicable)
  - 5 min flexibility work

- **Friday** (Rest Day Activity)
  - Choose a favorite activity (swimming, dancing, cycling)

## Modifications for Different Fitness Levels
- **Beginner**: Reduce duration and intensity; focus on form.
- **Intermediate**: Add weights; increase reps and duration gradually.
- **Advanced**: Incorporate plyometrics; try resistance bands.

## Specific Recommendations to Combat Morning Tiredness
- Prioritize hydration upon waking.
- Light stretching routine before bed to promote sleep quality.
- Keep a consistent sleep schedule.
- Aim for at least 7-8 hours of sleep each night.
