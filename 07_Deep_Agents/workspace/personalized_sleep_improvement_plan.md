## Troubleshooting Common Challenges

### "I can't fall asleep without my phone"
- **Solution**: Replace with book or audiobook (low volume)
- **Transition**: Use blue light filter first week, then eliminate
- **Alternative**: Meditation apps on dedicated device with airplane mode

### "My bedtime varies due to work/social commitments"
- **Solution**: 80/20 rule - consistent 5-6 nights per week is still beneficial
- **Strategy**: Plan ahead and communicate boundaries
- **Compromise**: Maximum 1-hour deviation on special occasions

### "I still feel tired in the morning"
- **Check**: Are you getting 7-8 hours total sleep?
- **Evaluate**: Room environment (temperature, darkness, noise)
- **Consider**: Sleep disorders if no improvement after 4-6 weeks

## Emergency "Can't Sleep" Protocol

If you're lying awake for more than 20 minutes:
1. Get up and do quiet, non-stimulating activity
2. Read with dim light or practice relaxation
3. Return to bed when sleepy
4. Maintain wake time regardless of sleep quality
